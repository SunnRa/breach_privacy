Privacy Policy — Breach

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Breach ("we", "our", or "the app") is a data breach checker that allows you to find out whether your email address has been exposed in known data breaches. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Email address: When you perform a scan, the email address you enter is sent to our backend server solely to query the breach database. We do not store your email address on our servers after the query is completed.

Anonymous identifier: The app assigns you an anonymous, randomly generated ID (via Firebase Anonymous Authentication) to identify your session. This ID is not linked to your identity.

Push notification token: If you enable notifications, a device token is stored to allow us to send you breach alerts. This token is associated only with your anonymous ID.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To check your email address against the breach database (LeakCheck) and return results to you.
  • To send push notifications if you opt into breach monitoring (premium feature).

We do not sell, share, or rent your information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

LeakCheck: Breach queries are powered by LeakCheck (leakcheck.io). Your email is sent to their API to retrieve breach results. Please refer to LeakCheck's privacy policy for information on how they handle data.

Firebase (Google): We use Firebase for anonymous authentication and push notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging). Firebase may collect device-level data as described in Google's Firebase Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

Email addresses submitted for scanning are not stored after the query is returned. Your anonymous ID and notification token are retained only while you use the app and are deleted if you uninstall the app or request deletion.

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the email below. Since the app uses anonymous authentication, no personal account information is retained beyond what is described in this policy.

6. Children's Privacy

Breach is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
